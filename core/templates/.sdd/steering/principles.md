# SDD Principles

- Work from a spec, not from a vague prompt.
- Keep tasks small and reviewable.
- Do not mix unrelated changes.
- Do not change behavior unless authorized.
- Record decisions and deviations.
- Keep external writes explicit and auditable.
- Steering files represent current stable context.
- Significant changes to steering must be recorded in `.sdd/decisions/`.
- Do not assume all incoming work belongs to the current roadmap or main focus.
